Position at WW International (formerly Weight Watchers)




Product Designer (Coaching)





About WW
WW is a global wellness + technology company inspiring millions of people to adopt healthy habits for real life. We do this through engaging digital experiences, face-to-face workshops and sustainable programs that encompass healthy eating, physical activity and positive mindset. By drawing on over five decades of experience and expertise in behavioral science, we build communities in order to deliver wellness for all.



To learn more about WW and jobs with a purpose, visit ww.com .
About the role
W e are looking for a Product Designer to join our Coaching team. The Coaching team’s vision is to inspire WW members to reach their goals with support from coaches who’ve been there before. You’ll work to understand our coach’s behaviors, motivations and goals and design ways to help them deliver the best experience to our members.
Minimum requirements




2+ years design experience (outside of a school context)
Shipped one or more products
A portfolio with strong work and case studies with real, paid clients




About the team
Our Product Design team is a close-knit group of 30+ practitioners, each focused on Product Design, Visual Design and Experience research. The team is distributed between our offices in New York City and San Francisco.



We’re user-focused, believe deeply in user-centered design, and work in highly collaborat ive crews that include design-oriented engineers and user research minded product managers.



Our team is remarkably talented and growing fast, so this is an exciting time to join WW. The Health & Wellness space is growing exponentially and as a designer here, your work will have an immediate meaningful impact on the lives of our members across the globe.
About you




You focus on UX, understand how to gather product requirements, incorporate user research , frame the problem, focus on the user and articulate a clear design direction
You can prototyp e, make last-minute tweaks between user studies , know how to be polished as well as quick and dirty
You are able to navigate between exploration and tactical, iterative designs
You can analyze data, interviews, research and translate your insights into actionable layouts, prototyp es, and final designs
You understand analytics and can think and talk about the company’s bottom line
You believe in craft smanship and create elegant and compelling designs
You develop strong relationships with product managers, developers, and other designers
You thrive best when helping to define the requirements, not just translating them into designs
You can share shining examples of real work you’ve shipped, and what you’ve learned along the way
You facilitate and draw the best design ideas from teammates
